Privacy Policy for Postflow Pro

Last updated: March 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, connect social media profiles, or otherwise communicate with us. This includes:

  • Basic profile information (name, email address)
  • Authentication tokens for connected social platforms (TikTok, X, Meta, YouTube)
  • Content you choose to upload and publish through our service

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Publish content to your connected social media accounts on your behalf
  • Analyze usage patterns and improve the user experience

3. Authentication and Tokens

When you connect a social media account (like TikTok), we receive authorization tokens that allow us to post content on your behalf. We store these tokens securely and only use them to perform actions you explicitly request (like publishing a video). We do not use your social accounts to view, like, or comment on other content without your permission.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with third-party social platforms (like TikTok) when necessary to execute the publishing actions you request.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@postflow.pro (placeholder email).
